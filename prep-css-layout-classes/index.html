Scary Movies I saw this year

Smile

a really really scarey movie that has a unique take on how trauma manifests and festers within us. I haven't seen a film accurately portray inner trauma like this since Babadook. I saw this alone in a near empty theater and man I was so terrified, never again.

Barbarian

Initially released with huge hype then people hated on it because of said hype, Barbarian does what it set out to do very well. Ask me IRL and i'll tell you why it's good

Black Phone

Decent scary movie, my girlfriend laughed at me at one of the jumpscares.
